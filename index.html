Digital Economy in Europe

Study 2016 from eurostat

This statistic shows the growing importance of information and communication technologies (ICTs) of enterprises and households of all EU-28 states. The data is collected between 2011 and 2015 and originates from the “Digital economy and society statistics - enterprises” report from Eurostat.

Enterprises using social media

The purpose of social media among enterprises varies. The biggest shares go to social networks, blogs and microblogs, multimedia sharing platforms. As the use of wiki-based knowledge sharing tools decreases the remaining three grow more important over time.
